1. Service Agreement

These Terms and Conditions govern your use of Risa Medical services. By booking a consultation, you agree to be bound by these terms.

2. Services Provided

Risa Medical provides private healthcare services including:

  • General practice consultations
  • Women's health services
  • Weight management and wellness consultations
  • Alternative and holistic treatments
  • Minor surgical procedures
  • Telehealth consultations
  • Private prescriptions

3. Appointment Booking & Cancellation

Booking:

  • Appointments must be booked in advance
  • Payment is required at time of consultation
  • Valid ID must be presented at first appointment

Cancellation Policy:

  • 48 hours notice required for cancellations
  • Same-day cancellations incur full consultation fee
  • Emergency cancellations will be considered on individual basis
  • Repeated missed appointments may result in service termination

4. Fees & Payment

  • All fees are payable at time of consultation
  • Accepted payment methods: card, bank transfer, cash
  • Fees are non-refundable once service is provided
  • Additional charges may apply for extended consultations
  • Prescription fees are separate from consultation fees

5. Medical Records & Confidentiality

  • All consultations are strictly confidential
  • Medical records are maintained in accordance with GMC guidelines
  • Records may be shared only with explicit patient consent or legal requirement
  • Patients have right to access their medical records

6. Prescriptions

  • Prescriptions issued in accordance with MHRA guidelines
  • Patients responsible for collecting prescriptions from chosen pharmacy
  • Controlled substances require additional monitoring
  • Prescription renewals require appropriate follow-up consultations

7. Patient Responsibilities

Patients must:

  • Provide accurate and complete medical information
  • Attend appointments punctually
  • Follow treatment plans and medical advice
  • Report adverse reactions or concerns promptly
  • Respect clinic staff and other patients
  • Pay fees promptly

8. Emergency Care

  • Risa Medical is not an emergency service
  • For medical emergencies, call 999 or attend A&E
